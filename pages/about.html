About Me

Since I was young, I've been fascinated by technology and its potential to solve real-world problems. Growing up in Veldhoven, Netherlands, and later relocating to the UK, I found myself drawn to the intricacies of cyber technology and its implications, particularly in combating cybercrime. Watching police chases and delving into the world of crimes further fuelled my curiosity, ultimately leading me to pursue a degree in Computer Science. As a 2nd Year Computer Science student, I'm dedicated to honing my skills to contribute positively to society's digital landscape.

During my journey, I've also gained practical experience through work placements at Fujitsu and Vodafone. These opportunities provided me with invaluable insights into the industry, allowing me to apply my academic knowledge to real-world projects and further refine my skills.

Featured Projects

2D RPG

Tourism website

Created a tourism website for my hometown.

When I was tasked with designing a tourism website for my hometown, I was determined not just to highlight its stunning surroundings but also to capture the essence of its cultural history. Drawing influence from local landmarks and historical locations, I carefully designed each page to provide visitors with an immersive experience. Overcoming technical obstacles like optimising site performance and guaranteeing mobile responsiveness helped me improve my skills as a web developer.

When I began developing a 2D RPG game based on a popular webcomic, I was motivated by a desire to engage players in a captivating plot while also providing an engaging gameplay experience. Using Unity's developed game development tools, I carefully created every component of the game, from character design to interactive gameplay mechanisms. One of the most exciting challenges I faced was translating the rich narrative and dynamic characters from the webcomic into a playable game environment. This required a careful balance between being authentic to the source material and including elements of interactivity and player agency. I developed the game mechanics through iterative design and playtesting to ensure that narration and gameplay flowed seamlessly together.
